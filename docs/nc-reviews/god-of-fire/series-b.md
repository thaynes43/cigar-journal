## Review 1 - Double Robusto - 11/16/2025

Well constructed and easy to like expensive stick. Starts off like a spicy hot chocolate which I guess is sometimes known as a Mexican cocoa. Right from the bat I can see this is going to be a complex one and was a great choice for a NC after my CC binge. Some cedar notes mixed into the silky smooth smoke. Raisin and brown sugar come in as the first third transitions into the second. Smoke is heavier now too. Getting to the end of the second third yields some hefty chocolate notes with some lingering sweetness of dry fruit. The smoke is plentiful but drier now, less creamy.
