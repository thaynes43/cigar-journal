# Cheat Sheet

## Tobacco Types & Tasting Notes

### Wrapper Types

**Connecticut Shade**
- Light, creamy, mild flavor
- Notes: Hay, cedar, cream, light spice

**Connecticut Broadleaf**
- Rich, earthy, full-bodied
- Notes: Chocolate, coffee, earth, leather

**Habanos (Cuban)**
- Medium to full, complex
- Notes: Cedar, honey, citrus, earth, floral

**Maduro**
- Dark, sweet, rich
- Notes: Dark chocolate, coffee, molasses, earth

**Corojo**
- Spicy, full-bodied
- Notes: Black pepper, spice, wood, leather

**Criollo**
- Medium-bodied, balanced
- Notes: Cedar, nuts, light spice, cream

**Candela**
- Light, grassy, mild
- Notes: Grass, hay, light sweetness

### Binder & Filler Origins

**Nicaragua**
- Full-bodied, spicy, bold
- Notes: Black pepper, earth, wood, cocoa

**Dominican Republic**
- Medium-bodied, smooth, balanced
- Notes: Cedar, nuts, cream, light spice

**Honduras**
- Medium to full, rich
- Notes: Earth, wood, spice, cocoa

**Cuba**
- Medium to full, complex
- Notes: Cedar, honey, citrus, earth, floral, spice

**Ecuador**
- Medium-bodied wrapper source
- Notes: Cedar, cream, nuts, light spice

**Mexico**
- Earthy, rich
- Notes: Earth, coffee, dark chocolate, spice

**Brazil**
- Full-bodied, rich
- Notes: Earth, coffee, dark chocolate, spice
